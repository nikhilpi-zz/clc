How it works

Our Services

Logos

With a DesignWorks logo you can transform your department, business, or student group into a household name! Everyone will associate you with your head-turning insignia, and your logo will help turn your group into the brand you always knew you could be. Our final deliverable will be a logo with a transparent background rasterized in multiple dimensions and a .ai and/or .eps file for your future use.

Flyers

Do you have an event or info session coming up, but don’t know how to advertise it properly? Our talented designers know what designs catch the eye, and your flyer is guaranteed to attract the attention of passer-bys. Our designers can make flyers of all sizes, and can do anything ranging from illustrations to geometric designs in order to advertise your event!

Social Media Package

With the DesignWorks social media package your department, business, or student group will have the online presence you always hoped to have! You’ll receive a high resolution Facebook profile picture and cover photo that you can use not only for Facebook events and pages but also for other social media platforms like twitter and instagram. Our designers will optimize sizes and resolutions for upload compression and orientation.

Pamphletes

Our designers can create templates for pamphlets of any kind! If you have a word document full of important information, your DesignWorks pamphlet will make your content visually appealing and easy to read!
